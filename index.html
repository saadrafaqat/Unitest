Organizations

Owner Admin, College Admins & Students

Two admin tiers: Platform Owner sees everything (manages colleges, private students) but not college student progress. College Admins see only their own college students' progress.

Private Students

Personal dashboard, individual mock tests, progress tracking, notes, lectures and public community chat. Managed by Platform Owner.

College Students

College-only login, college chat rooms, shared resources, progress tracking. Their progress is visible only to their College Admin.

Book Demo

College Admin

Track batch progress, manage students, review attempts and monitor performance — only for their own college. Cannot see private students or other colleges.

Privacy separation: College users only see their own college workspace. Private users only see the private/public side. Platform Owner (you) can manage all colleges and private students, see private student progress, but cannot see college student progress — that's only visible to each College Admin.
