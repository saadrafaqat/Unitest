Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about NUST NET and our platform

The NUST Entry Test (NET) is a mandatory standardized test for admission to all undergraduate programs at NUST. It is conducted multiple times a year.

NUST aggregate for Engineering: NET (75%) + SSC (10%) + HSSC (15%). Use our Aggregate Calculator for accurate results.

NET Engineering: 200 marks (Math 100, Physics 60, English 40). +1 for correct, -0.25 for wrong.
