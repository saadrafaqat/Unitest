About UniTest

Built by students, trusted by colleges across Pakistan

UniTest started with a simple mission — make high-quality entry test preparation accessible to every Pakistani student aiming for the country's top universities. From NUST to MDCAT, from UET to FAST, our platform brings authentic mock tests, real exam patterns, and verified aggregate calculations under one roof.

  • 10+ Universities Covered — NUST, MDCAT, UET, FAST, GIKI, PIEAS, COMSATS, NED, Air & more
  • Authentic Test Patterns — Each exam mirrors the real marking scheme, duration, and structure
  • For Students & Colleges — Private aspirants get full access; colleges get their own private workspaces
  • Privacy by Design — College data stays inside the college. Private users stay private.
